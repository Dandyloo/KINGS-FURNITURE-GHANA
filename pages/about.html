Who we are

Built in Takoradi.
Trusted across Ghana.

"We didn't set out to build a furniture company. We set out to build furniture worth keeping."

Kings Furniture Ghana was founded in 1992 in Takoradi by a craftsman with a simple belief — that Ghanaian homes deserved furniture built to the same standard as the world's finest. What began as a small workshop on the western coast has grown into the country's most recognised furniture brand.

Every piece we make starts with the same question: will this still be beautiful in thirty years? We source hardwoods with patience, join them with precision, and finish them with the kind of care that only comes from people who genuinely love their work.

We serve homeowners, architects, hoteliers and institutions across Ghana — from Takoradi to Accra, Kumasi to Tamale. Our workshop has grown, but our standard has never changed.
